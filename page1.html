Taylor Swift's Discography

This shows the different songs that Taylor Swift has released during her entire music career.


Taylor Swift (2006)

Taylor Swift is the self-titled debut album of Taylor Swift. It was recorded in 2005 and was released on October 24, 2006 by Big Machine Records. Taylor wrote/co-wrote 11 of the album's tracks, which were about her outlook on life as a teenager, dealing with romantic relationships, friendships, and insecurity. Taylor Swift is a country record with pop and pop rock elements, incorporating acoustic instruments such as guitars, banjos, and fiddles.
Source: Wikipedia

Fearless (2008/2021)
Fearless is the second album of Taylor Swift. It was recorded in 2008 and was released on November 11, 2008 by Big Machine Records. It was written largely by Taylor with seven of the thirteen writtenn by herself. She also co-produced all songs with Nathan Chapman, making this her debut as a record producer. Fearless is a country pop album featuring traditional country instruments such as banjos, fiddles, mandolins, and acoustic guitars, intertwined with electric guitars.
Source: Wikipedia
