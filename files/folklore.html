Folklore (2020)

Folklore is the eight studio album by Taylor. It was a surprise release on July 24, 2020, via Republic Records. Taylor wrote Folklore as "a collection of songs and stories that flowed like a stream of consciousness". It explores the themes of escapism, empathy, romance, and nostalgia. Taylor tells the story through a set of characters and fictional stories compared to her previous autobiographical style. This album consists of mellow ballads with neo-classical instruments, indie folk, alternative rock, and electroacoustic styles.
