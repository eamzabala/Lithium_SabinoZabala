Taylor Swift (2006)

Taylor Swift is the self-titled debut album of Taylor Swift. It was recorded in 2005 and was released on October 24, 2006 by Big Machine Records. Taylor wrote/co-wrote 11 of the album's tracks, which were about her outlook on life as a teenager, dealing with romantic relationships, friendships, and insecurity. Taylor Swift is a country record with pop and pop rock elements, incorporating acoustic instruments such as guitars, banjos, and fiddles.
